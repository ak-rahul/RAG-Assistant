# Sample Questions to Try

After uploading documents, try these questions:

## General Queries
- "What is this document about?"
- "Summarize the main points"
- "Give me an overview"

## Specific Information
- "What does section 3 discuss?"
- "Find information about [topic]"
- "When was [event] mentioned?"

## Comparison
- "Compare approaches in chapter 2 and 3"
- "What are the differences between X and Y?"

## Extraction
- "List all mentioned tools/technologies"
- "Extract all dates and deadlines"
- "What are the key recommendations?"

## Analysis
- "What are the pros and cons of [approach]?"
- "What challenges are identified?"
- "What solutions are proposed?"
